2019 R Bootcamp Schedule

Interested in attending?

Please register by completing this Google Form. We will ask for confirmation at a later date. Please note that space is limited. Please don’t sign-up if you you are uncertain about whether you will be able to attend.

Pre-bootcamp survey

Whether or not you want to attend the bootcamp, you are welcome to complete this anonymous (and light-hearted) survey. We’ll use the data drawn from it to show how one can use R and R Markdown to create reproducible data processing workflows and even full manuscripts.
